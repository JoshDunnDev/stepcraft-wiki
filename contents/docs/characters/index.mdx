# Character Races & Classes

In Stepcraft, your character's race and class define your starting abilities, appearance, and storyline. Each race has unique traits, starting locations, and quest lines that shape your adventure.

## Playable Races

### Elf

**Racial Traits:** 
- Natural affinity for nature magic and healing
- Enhanced agility and dexterity
- Starting bonus to Alchemy and Foraging skills
- Access to unique Elven quest line

**Background:** Elves are forest dwellers who live in harmony with nature. Your journey begins during the "Giving Days," a coming-of-age tradition where young elves prove themselves to their community. As an elf, you'll start your adventure in the Heart Grove under the guidance of Elder Mirae.

**Racial Quest Line:** The Giving Begins → Festival Preparations → From Field to Feast → Storm Damage → The Herbalist's Need → Mushrooms and Medicine → A Cry for Help → The Ruins' Secret → The Gleaming Prophecy → The Elder's Wisdom

### Starting Stats
All characters begin with base stats that can be improved through gameplay:
- **Health Points (HP):** Your character's life force
- **Attack:** Physical damage dealt in combat
- **Defense:** Resistance to incoming damage
- **Agility:** Movement speed and dodge chance
- **Critical Chance:** Probability of dealing critical damage
