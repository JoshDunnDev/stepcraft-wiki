## Elves - The Forest Guardians

<div className="flex flex-col lg:flex-row gap-8 my-8">
  <div className="lg:w-1/3">

      <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
        <div><strong>Lifespan:</strong> 800-1200 years</div>
        <div><strong>Height:</strong> 5'6" - 6'2"</div>
        <div><strong>Build:</strong> Slender and graceful</div>
        <div><strong>Hair:</strong> Often silver, gold, or auburn</div>
        <div><strong>Eyes:</strong> Green, blue, or violet</div>

    </div>

  <div className="lg:w-2/3">
    ### History & Culture
    
    Elves are the ancient guardians of Willow's Reach, a mystical forest realm where nature and magic intertwine. For millennia, they have lived in perfect harmony with their environment, developing a deep understanding of natural cycles, healing arts, and the delicate balance that sustains all life.
    
    Their society is built around the **Heart Grove**, a sacred grove centered on an enormous World Tree whose roots span the entire forest. This tree is not merely a landmarkâ€”it's a living connection to the natural world that guides elven philosophy and decision-making.
    
    ### The Giving Days Tradition
    
    Every young elf must complete the **Giving Days**, a coming-of-age ritual where they prove their worth to the community through acts of service. This tradition teaches that true strength comes not from individual achievement, but from contribution to the greater good.
    
    ### Racial Traits & Abilities
    
    **Natural Affinity:**
    - **Enhanced Alchemy** - Start with bonus experience in potion brewing
    - **Foraging Mastery** - Natural ability to find rare herbs and mushrooms
    - **Nature's Blessing** - Increased health regeneration in forest environments
    - **Keen Senses** - Better detection of hidden resources and secrets
    
    **Combat Specialization:**
    - **Agility Focus** - Natural grace provides dodge bonuses
    - **Archery Expertise** - Traditional elven weapon training
    - **Defensive Magic** - Access to protective nature spells
    
    **Social Benefits:**
    - **Forest Network** - Better prices with nature-aligned vendors
    - **Ancient Wisdom** - Access to unique dialogue options
    - **Diplomatic Heritage** - Bonus reputation with neutral factions
    
    ### Starting Location: Willow's Reach
    
    Begin your journey in the mystical forests of Willow's Reach, where ancient trees whisper secrets and magical energy flows through every leaf. Your adventure starts at the Heart Grove under the guidance of Elder Mirae, the wise leader of your community.
    
    **Key Starting Areas:**
    - **Heart Grove** - Sacred center and quest hub
    - **Willowbend Plaza** - Trading post and crafting stations
    - **Blooming Glade** - Agricultural learning area
    - **Mycelwood Thicket** - Foraging and alchemy training

## Humans - The Desert Innovators

<div className="flex flex-col lg:flex-row gap-8 my-8">
  <div className="lg:w-1/3">
    <img src="/assets/avatars/races/human.png" alt="Human Race" className="w-full max-w-sm mx-auto rounded-xl border-4 border-blue-200 shadow-lg" />
    
    <div className="mt-6 bg-blue-50 dark:bg-blue-900/20 p-4 rounded-lg">
      <h4 className="font-bold text-blue-800 dark:text-blue-200 mb-3">Quick Facts</h4>
      <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
        <div><strong>Lifespan:</strong> 60-80 years</div>
        <div><strong>Height:</strong> 5'4" - 6'0"</div>
        <div><strong>Build:</strong> Varied and adaptable</div>
        <div><strong>Hair:</strong> All natural colors</div>
        <div><strong>Eyes:</strong> Brown, blue, green, hazel</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  
  <div className="lg:w-2/3">
    ### History & Culture
    
    Humans have claimed the challenging **Sunforge Sands** as their domain, transforming a harsh desert landscape into a thriving center of trade and innovation. Through ingenuity, cooperation, and relentless determination, they have built magnificent cities around life-giving oases.
    
    Their society values **progress through unity**â€”the belief that by working together and sharing knowledge, humanity can overcome any obstacle. This philosophy has made them natural traders, diplomats, and inventors.
    
    ### The Merchant's Calling
    
    Human culture revolves around the great **Trade Routes** that connect distant lands. Young humans often begin their adult lives by joining merchant caravans, learning the ways of commerce, negotiation, and the diverse cultures they encounter.
    
    ### Racial Traits & Abilities
    
    **Adaptability:**
    - **Quick Learner** - Gain experience 10% faster in all skills
    - **Jack of All Trades** - No skill penalties, balanced growth
    - **Heat Resistance** - Reduced damage from fire and desert conditions
    - **Resource Efficiency** - Tools and equipment last longer
    
    **Combat Specialization:**
    - **Weapon Versatility** - Can effectively use any weapon type
    - **Tactical Mind** - Bonus to combat strategy and formation fighting
    - **Endurance Training** - Higher stamina and longer combat duration
    
    **Social Benefits:**
    - **Born Traders** - Better prices with all merchants
    - **Cultural Bridge** - Easier reputation gains with all races
    - **Leadership Qualities** - Bonus to group activities and quests
    
    ### Starting Location: Sunforge Sands
    
    *Coming with the Human expansion - Under Development*
    
    Begin your adventure in the bustling oasis cities of Sunforge Sands, where merchants from across the world gather to trade exotic goods and share tales of distant lands.
    
    ### Future Content
    
    The human storyline and territories are currently under development. Stay tuned for:
    - Merchant guild questlines
    - Desert exploration and survival
    - Trade route management
    - Innovation and invention systems
    - Cross-cultural diplomacy challenges
  </div>
</div>

## Dwarves - The Mountain Crafters

    <div className="mt-6 bg-orange-50 dark:bg-orange-900/20 p-4 rounded-lg">
      <h4 className="font-bold text-orange-800 dark:text-orange-200 mb-3">Quick Facts</h4>
      <div className="space-y-2 text-sm">
        <div><strong>Lifespan:</strong> 200-300 years</div>
        <div><strong>Height:</strong> 4'0" - 4'8"</div>
        <div><strong>Build:</strong> Stocky and muscular</div>
        <div><strong>Hair:</strong> Usually braided, all colors</div>
        <div><strong>Eyes:</strong> Brown, amber, gray</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  
  <div className="lg:w-2/3">
    ### History & Culture
    
    Dwarves have made the treacherous **Ironheart Cliffs** their ancestral home, carving magnificent cities directly into the mountain faces. Masters of engineering and craftsmanship, they have turned solid rock into works of art that rival nature's own creations.
    
    Their culture is built on the principle of **strength through craft**â€”the belief that true power comes not from conquest, but from the ability to create, build, and improve. Every dwarf takes immense pride in their chosen craft, whether it's smithing, engineering, or gem-cutting.
    
    ### The Forge Masters
    
    Dwarven society is organized around the **Great Forges**, massive workshop complexes that never cease their production. These forges are not just workplacesâ€”they're centers of learning, tradition, and community life.
    
    ### Racial Traits & Abilities
    
    **Crafting Mastery:**
    - **Master Smiths** - Exceptional bonus to smithing and metallurgy
    - **Gem Expertise** - Enhanced ability to find and cut precious stones
    - **Engineering Genius** - Access to advanced construction and mechanisms
    - **Tool Mastery** - All crafting tools are 50% more effective
    
    **Combat Specialization:**
    - **Heavy Armor Proficiency** - No movement penalties from armor
    - **Axe and Hammer Mastery** - Traditional dwarven weapon expertise
    - **Mountain Endurance** - Resistance to physical damage and fatigue
    
    **Social Benefits:**
    - **Craftsman's Network** - Exceptional prices for crafted goods
    - **Ancient Grudges** - Complex relationships with other races
    - **Clan Honor** - Reputation affects entire communities
    
    ### Starting Location: Ironheart Cliffs
    
    *Coming with the Dwarf expansion - Under Development*
    
    Begin in the magnificent mountain cities of Ironheart Cliffs, where the ring of hammers on anvils echoes through carved stone halls, and every surface tells the story of masterful craftsmanship.
    
    ### Future Content
    
    The dwarven experience is currently in development:
    - Clan-based questlines and politics
    - Advanced crafting and engineering systems
    - Underground exploration and mining expeditions
    - Ancient dwarven mysteries and lost technologies
    - Inter-clan relationships and rivalries
  </div>

    Orcs inhabit the mystical **Moonmere**, a realm of wetlands and marshes where the boundaries between the physical and spiritual worlds blur. Far from the savage stereotype, orcs are deeply spiritual people who follow ancient codes of honor and respect.
    
    Their philosophy of **honor through struggle** teaches that true strength is forged in adversity, and that facing challenges head-onâ€”whether in combat, nature, or lifeâ€”builds character and wisdom.
    
    ### The Spirit Walkers
    
    Orcish culture is guided by **Spirit Walkers**, shamanic leaders who commune with the ancestors and natural spirits that dwell in Moonmere's mists. These spiritual guides help maintain the balance between the living and the dead.
    
    ### Racial Traits & Abilities
    
    **Spiritual Connection:**
    - **Spirit Sight** - Can perceive hidden magical auras and spiritual entities
    - **Ancestral Wisdom** - Access to ancient knowledge and forgotten lore
    - **Marsh Navigation** - No movement penalties in wetland environments
    - **Night Vision** - Enhanced sight in darkness and low light
    
    **Combat Specialization:**
    - **Berserker Rage** - Temporary massive damage increase when health is low
    - **Intimidation** - Natural fear effect on weaker enemies
    - **Weapon Improvisation** - Can effectively use improvised weapons
    
    **Social Benefits:**
    - **Tribal Bonds** - Strong loyalty systems within orc communities
    - **Honor Code** - Reputation system based on deeds and challenges
    - **Spiritual Guidance** - Access to unique shamanic questlines
    
    ### Starting Location: Moonmere

    Begin your journey in the misty wetlands of Moonmere, where ancient spirits walk among the living and every challenge is an opportunity to prove your worth and honor.

    - Inter-tribal politics and alliance building
