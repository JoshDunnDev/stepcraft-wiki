Discover all the creatures you'll face in your Stepcraft adventures. From humble slimes to mighty golems, each enemy presents unique challenges and rewards.

## Enemy Tiers

## Combat Strategy

Each enemy tier requires different strategies and equipment:

- **T0-T1**: Basic equipment and straightforward combat
- **T2-T3**: Upgraded equipment and tactical awareness
- **T4-T5**: High-tier equipment and advanced combat techniques
- **T6**: End-game equipment and mastery of all combat mechanics
