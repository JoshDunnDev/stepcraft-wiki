## Combat Information

### Recommended Player Stats
For comfortable combat against this enemy, players should have approximately:
- **~480 HP, 175 ATK, 95 DEF**

### Combat Tips
- Ghosts can phase through attacks occasionally
- Be patient and wait for clear openings\n- Ensure your equipment is upgraded
- Stock up on healing potions
