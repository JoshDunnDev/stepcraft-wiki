## Combat Information

### Recommended Player Stats
For comfortable combat against this enemy, players should have approximately:
- **~230 HP, 60 ATK, 38 DEF**

### Combat Tips
- Mushrooms are stationary but hit hard when you get close
- Use ranged attacks when possible or hit-and-run tactics\n- Good for learning basic combat mechanics
- Basic equipment should suffice

## Tier Information

**T1** enemies are designed for players with **~230 HP, 60 ATK, 38 DEF**. Low-tier enemies that require basic combat skills and decent equipment.
