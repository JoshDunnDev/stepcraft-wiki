## Combat Information

### Recommended Player Stats
For comfortable combat against this enemy, players should have approximately:
- **~300 HP, 95 ATK, 60 DEF**

### Combat Tips
- Rats are fast and aggressive, requiring quick reflexes
- Focus on timing your attacks between their rushes\n- Ensure your equipment is upgraded
- Stock up on healing potions
