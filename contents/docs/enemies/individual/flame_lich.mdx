## Combat Information

### Recommended Player Stats
For comfortable combat against this enemy, players should have approximately:
- **~900 HP, 370 ATK, 180 DEF**

### Combat Tips
- Liches are powerful spellcasters with high damage
- Interrupt their casting when possible and use hit-and-run tactics\n- High-tier equipment is essential
- Master your combat skills before engaging
- Consider using combat potions for buffs
