## Combat Information

### Recommended Player Stats
For comfortable combat against this enemy, players should have approximately:
- **~480 HP, 175 ATK, 95 DEF**

### Combat Tips
- Slimes have moderate health but low defense, making them vulnerable to sustained attacks
- Watch for their bouncing movement patterns\n- Ensure your equipment is upgraded
- Stock up on healing potions

## Tier Information

**T3** enemies are designed for players with **~480 HP, 175 ATK, 95 DEF**. Challenging enemies that demand high-quality gear and combat experience.
