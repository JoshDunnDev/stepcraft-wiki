## Combat Information

### Recommended Player Stats
For comfortable combat against this enemy, players should have approximately:
- **~1200 HP, 525 ATK, 290 DEF**

### Combat Tips
- Golems have massive health and defense but attack slowly
- Focus on sustained damage and avoid their devastating attacks\n- Only attempt with the best available equipment
- Use all available combat buffs and consumables
