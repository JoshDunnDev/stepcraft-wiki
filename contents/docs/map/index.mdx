## Overview

The world is divided into distinct regions, each with its own character, resources, and activities. Some locations have both main areas and outskirt regions that offer different levels of resources and challenges.

## Activities by Skill

### Fishing Locations
- [Silverleaf Refuge](/docs/map/individual/silverleaf_refuge) - Salmon, catfish, bass, and rare eldritch bass
- [Verdant Isle](/docs/map/individual/verdant_isle) - Flying fish, octopus, pufferfish, and cycloptopus
- [Pearlsand Strand](/docs/map/individual/pearlsand_strand_docks) - Deep-water catches like swordfish and lobster
- [Crystalrun River](/docs/map/individual/crystalrun_river) - Trout, pike, and mystical sea snakes
- [Moonmere](/docs/map/individual/moonmere) - Lake fish including tilapia and rainbow fish

### Mining Locations
- [Titan's Rest](/docs/map/individual/titans_rest) - Basic ores: stone, copper, iron, red ore
- [Ironheart Cliffs](/docs/map/individual/ironheart_cliffs) - Rare ores: silver, gold, blue ore, red ore

### Foraging Areas
- [Crimson Garden](/docs/map/individual/crimson_garden) - Exotic fruits and berries
- [Verdant Isle](/docs/map/individual/verdant_isle) - Tropical fruits: pear, lemon, kiwi, apricot
- [Pearlsand Strand](/docs/map/individual/pearlsand_strand) - Coastal finds: banana, coconut, starfish
- [Moonmere](/docs/map/individual/moonmere) - Complete variety of forageable items

### Combat Zones
- [Thornspire Ruins](/docs/map/individual/thornspire_ruins) - Ghosts and rats
- [Hollow Crown](/docs/map/individual/hollow_crown) - Slimes, mushrooms, and ents
- [Sunforge Sands](/docs/map/individual/sunforge_sands) - Elemental beings and golems

### Crafting Hubs
- [Willowbend Plaza](/docs/map/individual/willowbend_plaza) - All crafting skills available
- [Hollow Crown](/docs/map/individual/hollow_crown) - Crafting and alchemy in ancient workshops
- [Sunforge Sands](/docs/map/individual/sunforge_sands) - Advanced crafting in harsh conditions
