## Resource Categories

### Crafting Materials

**Tool Parts & Components**
- Essential components for crafting advanced tools
- Includes toolheads from copper to red tier
- Used in smithing and tool assembly

**Ores & Raw Materials** 
- Base materials extracted through mining
- Stone, copper, iron, silver, gold, blue, and red ores
- Foundation for most crafting recipes

**Refined Materials**
- Bars: Processed metals ready for crafting
- Planks: Processed wood for construction and tools
- Handles: Specialized components for tool assembly

### Natural Resources

**Gems & Precious Materials**
- 18 different gems for trinketry and decoration
- Used in creating rings, amulets, and high-tier equipment
- Valuable trading commodities

**Logs & Wood**
- Raw wood materials from different tree types
- Essential for carpentry and construction
- Fuel source for various crafting processes

### Food & Consumables

**Fish** (Largest Category - 57 items)
- Comprehensive variety of fish species
- Primary food source and trading commodity
- Different rarity and value tiers

**Farming Products**
- 21 different crops and agricultural products
- Sustainable food sources
- Ingredients for cooking and alchemy

**Foraging Items**
- 29 wild-gathered resources
- Seasonal and location-specific items
- Mix of food and crafting materials

### Specialized Resources

**Hunting Materials**
- 28 different hunting-related resources
- Animal products and combat materials
- Used for equipment and consumables

**Combat Resources**
- Specialized materials for warfare
- Ammunition and tactical supplies

**Ingredients & Miscellaneous**
- Unique crafting components
- Special-purpose materials
- Quest-related items
